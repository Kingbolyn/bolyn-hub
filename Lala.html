Our Story

Lala's Finery was founded in 2017 by Master Barber Lala Adeyemi, who learned the craft from his grandfather in Ibadan. What began as a single chair in a small shop in Yaba has grown into Lagos' most prestigious barbershop, with three locations across the city.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that a great haircut is more than just grooming — it's a transformation. Every client who sits in our chair receives a personalized consultation, precision cutting, and styling advice tailored to their lifestyle.

We invest in continuous training for our barbers, ensuring they master both classic techniques and the latest trends. Our shop features premium products from American Crew, Layrite, and our own Lala's Finery line.
