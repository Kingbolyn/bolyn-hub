Our Story

TT Scents was founded in 2020 with a singular mission: to bring authentic luxury fragrances to Nigeria at fair prices. We recognized that discerning Nigerian men and women deserved access to the world's finest perfumes without the inflated costs of grey market imports.

We partner directly with authorized distributors of Creed, Tom Ford, Chanel, Dior, and over 50 other prestigious houses. Every bottle in our collection is 100% authentic, with full batch codes and packaging.

Our team of fragrance consultants helps clients discover their signature scent through personalized consultations, sample programs, and our exclusive "Scent Journey" experience.
