About Jedinna

Jedinna is a premium design studio based in Lagos, Nigeria, founded in 2019 by a team of designers who believed African digital products deserved world-class design. We've since grown into a team of 12 designers, researchers, and strategists serving clients across 4 continents.

Our approach combines deep user research with bold creative vision. We don't just make things look good — we design experiences that solve real problems and drive measurable business outcomes.

Whether you're a startup seeking product-market fit or an enterprise optimizing user engagement, we bring the same level of craft and commitment to every project.
