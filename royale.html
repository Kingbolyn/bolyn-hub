Our Story

Royale Bite was conceived in 2018 by Chef Emmanuel Bolyn, a culinary artist trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris, with a vision to bring world-class fine dining to Lagos. What started as a intimate 12-table restaurant in Victoria Island has evolved into one of Nigeria's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients from around the world — Japanese Wagyu, French truffles, Italian burrata — and prepare them with techniques honed over decades, while infusing each dish with Nigerian hospitality and warmth.

With a team of 8 internationally trained chefs, a 5,000-bottle wine cellar, and private dining rooms that have hosted presidents and celebrities, Royale Bite continues to set the standard for luxury dining in West Africa.

Emmanuel Bolyn

Executive Chef & Founder
